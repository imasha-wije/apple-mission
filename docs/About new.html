Introduction for Mental Health

There is probably no one who has not heard the word "crazy", so much. so that the word "crazy" has become a joke in society. But if someone is really "crazy", the situation is completely different. Since then, the whole society looks at that person as a strange animal, and the knowledge and understanding of the people in our country about mental health is minimal. Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make healthy choices.
